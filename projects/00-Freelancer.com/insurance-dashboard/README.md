## **Insurance Claims Dashboard (Tableau)**
-	Analyzed 7,647 insurance claim records and delivered interactive Tableau dashboards covering demographics, occupation, car types, and risk factors.
-	Built multiple analytical visualizations (bar/line charts, word cloud, running totals) and calculated fields for claim cost analysis.
-	Produced a full Tableau Story summarizing $42M total claim value, claim frequency patterns, and high-risk customer segmentation.
